About The Founder

Founder of the Message of Humanity movement.

Maulana Syed Abul Hasan Ali Nadvi (1914–1999) was an illustrious thinker, writer, educationist, social reformer, and international traveller who stood for mutual respect, peaceful coexistence, human values, tolerance, and harmony.

Mission

To repeat and strengthen the Message of Humanity through programs that serve people directly, while preserving the founder’s legacy and the institution’s public record for future generations.

Volunteer ethos and finance

The organization is volunteer-driven and community-supported. Its model depends on service, trust, and careful stewardship rather than commercial fundraising.

Founder biography

Maulana Syed Abul Hasan Ali Nadvi, known widely as Ali Miyan, inspired the Forum through his lifelong emphasis on humanity, harmony, and moral responsibility. His work bridged scholarship, public engagement, and social reform.

A concise biography, milestone chronology, and access to archival materials help frame the founder’s legacy as an active institutional foundation rather than a ceremonial memory.
